# Privacy Policy for Duck Note

*Last updated: May 28, 2026*

Duck Note ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a lightweight Chrome extension designed to help you take notes easily across browser tabs. We value your privacy, and our practices are built around a core principle: **we do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data.**

---

## 1. Information Collected and Stored

Duck Note handles only the information necessary to provide its core features. This information is limited to:
* **Note Content**: The text content you type into the sticky note textarea.
* **Layout and Preference States**: The X and Y coordinates of the note on your screen, the theme color selection, and whether the note card is collapsed or expanded.

## 2. Data Storage and Syncing

All data handled by Duck Note is saved on your device using Chrome's extension API (`chrome.storage.sync`):
* **On-Device Storage**: Your notes and preferences remain inside your browser's secure sandboxed storage.
* **Syncing**: If you are signed into Google Chrome with sync enabled, Chrome will synchronize your preferences and note content across your authorized devices (e.g. between your laptop and desktop). This synchronization is managed entirely by Google's secure browser infrastructure.
* **No Server Storage**: We do not host or operate any external servers. Your notes are never transmitted to us or any external third parties.

## 3. Third-Party Services and Analytics

* **No Analytics/Telemetry**: We do not track how you use the extension. There are no tracking scripts, cookies, or analytics libraries (like Google Analytics) bundled inside this extension.
* **No Third-Party APIs**: The extension does not make any external network requests or fetch data from third-party APIs.

## 4. User Control

Because all note data is stored locally, you have total control:
* **Deletion**: You can delete your notes and reset all preference data at any time by clicking the cross button (**×**) in the Duck Note header. This instantly clears the extension's sync storage namespace on your browser.
* **Extension Removal**: Uninstalling the Duck Note extension will automatically delete all stored note content and positioning configurations from your device.
